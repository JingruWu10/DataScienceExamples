1. Brief description of your data set (age of the data, number of observations and attributes)
The data set has been created in 1996, with total of 3,974,305 observations and 15 attributes.
2. At least 1 yes-no or binary-choice question (Is it...? Does it...?)
The data answers:
Is there gender discrepancy in low income group/ high income group?
Is there difference among different native country group when talking about income?
Do university graduates earn more than high school graduates?
3. At least 1 non-binary question (What is...? How many...? When does...?)
What is the relationship between education and income?
what is the relationship between marital status and income?
Which age group earns most?
4. Future improvements to the data set: What other related data could be collected?
A longer period of data could be used to see the trend of the population. If the median age for a population is getting older? In addition, with a longer period of data, we could analysis if people are moving to other states or countries and predict which state or city will be populated in the next 10 years.
